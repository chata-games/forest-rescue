# Tileable ashfall scar floor material

Seamless tileable charred forest ground texture with ash, ember specks, and scorched earth. Painterly Forest Rescue style.

- 128x128 tileable, no objects, no path
- Top-down view
- Dark gray-brown palette with orange ember accents

References: gameplay-mockup.png (palette), material-sawmill-floor.png (tile style)
