# Tileable sawmill floor material

Seamless tileable industrial sawmill clearing ground texture with wood chips, sawdust, and rust-stained packed dirt. Painterly Forest Rescue style.

- 128x128 tileable, no objects, no path
- Top-down view
- Warm brown-orange industrial palette
